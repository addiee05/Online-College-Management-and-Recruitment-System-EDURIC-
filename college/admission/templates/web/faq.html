FAQs - College Management & Recruitment System

Your questions, answered.

General Questions

What is the Online College Management and Recruitment System?

This system is a platform designed to manage college administration tasks, student enrollment, recruitment processes, and related activities efficiently.

Who can access this system?

The system is accessible to administrators, faculty, students, and recruiters, each with specific roles and privileges.

Student FAQs

How do I register as a new student?

You can register by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling in the required details. Once submitted, your account will be verified by the admin team.

Where can I view my course schedule?

After logging in, navigate to the "Dashboard" section and click on "My Schedule" to view your course timetable.

Recruiter FAQs

How can I post a job for recruitment?

Log in to your recruiter account and navigate to the "Post a Job" section. Fill in the job details and submit it for approval by the admin.

Can I filter applicants based on specific criteria?

Yes, you can use the advanced filters in the "Applicants" section to shortlist candidates based on qualifications, skills, or experience.

Technical Support

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How can I contact technical support?

For any technical issues, you can reach our support team at support@collegemanagement.com.
